How to install Nestly on your Android phone
============================================

1. Download the APK
   - Go to https://github.com/tanakaprince49-cell/NESTLYY/releases
   - Find the latest release and download nestly-preview.apk

2. Allow installation from unknown sources
   - When you open the APK, Android will ask you to allow installing apps
     from this source (your browser or file manager)
   - Tap "Settings" and enable "Allow from this source"
   - Go back and tap "Install"

3. Open the app
   - Find "Nestly" in your app drawer and open it
   - Sign in with Google, email, or continue anonymously

Requirements
- Android 8.0 or newer
- About 120 MB of storage

Updating
- When a new version is available, download the new APK from Releases
  and install it over the existing one. Your data will be kept.

Health Connect (optional)
- To sync weight, blood pressure, and sleep with other health apps:
  1. Install "Health Connect by Google" from Play Store (if not pre-installed)
  2. Open Nestly -> Settings -> Health Connect -> Connect
  3. Grant the requested permissions

Troubleshooting
- "App not installed": Make sure you don't have a different version
  of Nestly already installed. Uninstall it first, then try again.
- "Blocked by Play Protect": Tap "More details" -> "Install anyway".
  This warning appears because the app isn't on Play Store yet.
